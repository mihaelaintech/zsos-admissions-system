Help

Quick guidance for using the admissions management system.

Student Guide

Students can log in, access their home page, and update their application details.

This includes phone number, campus city, study level, intake, application type, study mode, university choice, course selection, course duration, notes, and document tracking.

After making changes, click Save Application to update the record.

Staff Guide

Staff users can access the dashboard, search and filter applicants, add new records, edit applicant details, update workflow status, and delete records when permitted.

Managers have full access across all applicant records. Student advisers can view all applicants, but can edit and delete only the records they created.

Staff can also review document completion and internal notes for each applicant.
